What We Do

The Association for Computing Machinery (ACM) is the worlds largest scientific and educational computing society. ACM was founded in 1947 and is a not-for-profit professional membership group. The local branch welcomes you to join us in learning about the computing world.

Our ACM Security group is a local ACM branch whose mission is to help develop the cybersecurity profession for the student body of Missouri University of Science and Technology (MS&T) by sponsoring high-quality workshops and lectures from both local and national industry professionals, as well as hosting a variety of on-campus security events and competitions.
