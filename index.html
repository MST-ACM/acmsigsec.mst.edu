About SIG-Security

Our local ACM Special Interest Group on Security's mission is to help develop the cybersecurity profession for the student body of Missouri University of Science and Technology by sponsoring high-quality workshops and lectures from both local and national industry professionals, as well as hosting on campus security events and competitions.
