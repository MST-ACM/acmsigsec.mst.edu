Weekly Meetings

Every Monday 5-6 PM, in the ACM Security Discord

Come join us for our weekly ACM SIG-Security meeting! We talk about a variety of interesting security related subjects, provide beginner/medium/advanced level training, and get up to our elbows in hands-on activities. Come in any time during the meeting.

Upcoming Agenda: Personal Phone Security (9/7), Intro to Reverse Engineering (9/14), Malware Analysis (9/21), Cryptocurrency (9/28)

Wireless Workshop

Sat. Sept. 12, 1-4 PM, Loc: CS 207

Join us for a one-day event focused on teaching networking and wireless hacking. Bring your laptop and learn how to sniff traffic, crack encryption, deauth users, spoofing and much more! This includes hacking hardware tools (wireless card, LanTap Ninja Star) and a how-to manual that you can take home! The event requires no previous experience and will assume you know nothing about the hardware or the tools we will be using. The price is $45 per person and will cover all the necessary materials (Flier here).
