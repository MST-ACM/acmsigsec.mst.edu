Cyber Boot Camp

Cyber Boot Camp is a week long winter break trip to Albuquerque, New Mexico where you get hands on Cybersecurity training from both Sandia and Los Alamos National Labs. Along with being able to visit beautiful Albuquerque and Los Alamos, you will be trained in various subjects like Networking, Cryptography, Pen Testing, etc. Be sure to take part in this rare experience! Transportation will be provided though you may use your own means of transportation. Lodging will also be included in the registration fee.

The event requires no previous experience. If you have a Missouri drivers licence you will need to provide additional forms of identification.(Passport, Social Security Card, etc.)

Please contact Eric Michalak: esmxv3@mst.edu with any additional questions.
