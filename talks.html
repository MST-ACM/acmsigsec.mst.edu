Cantenna Build

Wednesday April 19th and Saturday April 22nd 2017

Computer Science Building, room 207, 7:00 PM Wednesday, 2:00 PM Saturday

Build a directional, external antenna for your computer out of a metal can! participants will work in groups. Then bring your new cantenna to the wireless security audit on April 22nd Where we race around Rolla searching for unsecured wirelessnetworks. Everyone is welcome, but acm members get priority

Preparation

Bring a metal can that is empty, free of ripples and dings, 3.25"-3.50" in diameter, and 5"-10" in length. $5.00 for parts( $3.00 for acm members) limited of cans available for $1.00 respectively.
