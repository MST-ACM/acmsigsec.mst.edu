Cyber Security Researcher - Chris Rawlings
Bio: A scientist and engineer, Chris’s expertise is network architecture and security, systems design and architecture, security engineering, and outreach & education. Recent graduate from Missouri University of Science & Technology with two BS degrees, one in Computer Engineering and the other in Computer Science. He has run Cyber Fire for the past two years, seeing several hundred people trained in the week-long programs in Washington D.C., Las Vegas, Denver, and San Diego. He is currently one of the instructors for LANL’s cyber security summer school program.


Abstract: A brief introduction to memory based incident response host forensics. This interactive and demo heavy presentation will show you some of the data, useful to cyber security incident responders, that is contained within memory and how to find traces of being compromised.
